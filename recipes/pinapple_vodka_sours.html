Big-Batch Pineapple-Basil Vodka Sours

Description:

Channel your inner mixologist with infused vodka and homemade sour mix, then shake them together for a sweet-tart sipper you can call your own.

Ingredients:

Pineapple-Basil Vodka

  • 1 1/2 cups chopped fresh pineapple
  • 1 (750-mL) bottle vodka
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, plus more for garnish

DIY Sour Mix

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup freshly-squeezed lime juice
  • 1 cup freshly-squeezed lemon juice

Additional

  • Ice as needed
  • Club soda as needed (optional)
  • Fresh pineapple wedges and fronds, for garnish

Directions:

  1. For pineapple-basil vodka, put pineapple in a 1-quart jar. Top with vodka, cover, and let stand until vodka is infused with pinapple flavor, 1 to 3 days. Add basil, cover and infuse for 1 to 2 hours; strain.

    Meanwhile, for the DIY sour mix, combine sugar and water in a saucepan and bring to a boil over high head, stirring until sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat; let cool. Add lime juice and lemon juice. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour and up to 1 week.

  2. In a pitcher, stir together pineapple-basil vodka and DIY sour mix. Serve in rocks glasses over ice. Top with club soda (if using); garnish with pineapple wedges, fronds, and additional basil.

Cook's Note:

Consider this the only vodka sour recipe you'll ever need. For a single cocktail, shake together 1 1/2 ounce pinapple-basil vodka and 1 1/2 ounce DIY sour mix in a shaker, then strain and serve over ice.
